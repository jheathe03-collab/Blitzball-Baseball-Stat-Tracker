Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?
Entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded or collected. Deleting the app deletes its data, so export a backup first if you want to keep it.
How do I move my data to another device?
Export a player, a whole season, or a tournament bracket as a file (from the Stats screens), send it to the other device (AirDrop, Files, Messages, iCloud Drive), then use the matching Import option in the app.
How do I share stats with my team?
Use “Export Spreadsheet (CSV)” from Season Stats or All Teams Stats to create a spreadsheet you can open in Numbers, Excel, or Google Sheets and share however you like.
I made a mistake during a game.
Use the Undo button on the live game screen, or tap the line-score cells and the bases diamond to correct plays directly.
Does the app need the internet?
No — it works completely offline.

Privacy Policy

Baseball StatTracker · Last updated: July 18, 2026

Baseball StatTracker (“the app”) is designed to be private. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information. In short: the app collects nothing and keeps all of your data on your own device.

Information we collect

None. The app does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. There is no account, no sign-up, and no login. We do not gather analytics, usage data, location, contacts, advertising identifiers, or any other information about you or your device.

How your data is stored

All content you create in the app — players, teams, games, seasons, tournaments, and statistics — is stored locally on your device using Apple’s on-device storage. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it. Deleting the app removes this data from your device.

Sharing and exporting

The app lets you export your stats as spreadsheet (CSV) files and share players, seasons, or tournament brackets as files. This only happens when you initiate it, and the file goes only to the destination you choose (for example AirDrop, Files, Messages, or another app). The developer never receives, stores, or has access to these files.

Third parties

The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software, and does not share any data with third parties. It works fully offline and makes no network requests to collect information.

Children’s privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new “last updated” date.
